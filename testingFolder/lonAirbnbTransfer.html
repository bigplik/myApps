Hello there - I would go train to St Pancras International and then change to the London Underground to London Bridge - then change to a mainline train to woolwich arsenal . There get bus 51 or 291 from outside the big TESCO. To my house stop called Plumstead common road/ Erindale terrace. Luton airport is 50 miles outside of London I’m afraid . There are other routes but this would be quickest for you I think! It’s also depends on what time of the day you arrive. Lots of main train stations have left luggage where you can store you bag for the day if you didn’t want to come in and out of London! Hope this helps! Mike
