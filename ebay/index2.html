P-Valve - This is one of the basic amenities for the comfort of a drysuit diving. 

Apart from the obvious benefits of the use of the drysuit, it brings one problem to solve. It is the need to urinate, which in the opinion of many people is the importance of intra-individual and so some people are able to abstain this need longer than others. However, in consequence, a matter of personal characteristics brings only postponed when the desire to urinate is no longer manageable. This time, in opposition to the theory of inter-individual urinate abstain ability comes thermo-inert diuresis phenomenon that occurs in each diver. In simple terms , it causes the blood outlet (pressure to ambient pressure) of the extremities and bladder pressure , resulting in a shorter time postponement of urination during the dive compared to the same process occurring on the surface.
